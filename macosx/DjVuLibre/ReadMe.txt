DjVu.mdimporter is a Spotlight metadata importer for DjVu files.  

To install:

   copy DjVu.mdimporter to /Library/Spotlight or ~/Library/Spotlight


The importer is based on the lastest stable build 
of djvulibre-3.5 at the time of compilation.  
It is deployed as a universal binary for ppc and i386 machines.

Further information on DjVu and DjVuLibre can be found at
http://djvulibre.djvuzone.org/.

DjVu.mdimporter and DjVuLibre.framework are maintained by
Jeff Sickel <jas@corpus-callosum.com>.
